ABOUT THE MINISTRY

"ERUOBAMI" is the name coined for the ministry as inspired by a deep encounter with the Holy Spirit. We are a gospel Ministry sent to the world to bring forth light and hope to the downcast, joy to the discouraged, and deliverance to the oppressed

We host both online and physical programmes. All our programmes and ministrations are uploaded on our social media platforms. Check the bottom of the page to access them

    Available for all church programmes - concerts, anniversaries, outreaches, etc
